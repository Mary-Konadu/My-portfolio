About Me

I'm Mary Konadu Nyarko. The first girl child of Mr. and Mrs. Nyarko born on the 2nd of March in the Asante Region of Ghana. Art being a desire from childhood led me ride from Makuva Prep. School(Accra), Great Alswel Basic School (Accra) with Creative Arts/Basic Design and Technology and Mathematics as my best subjects. The journey continued to Mfantsiman Girls' Senior High School (Saltpond) of which I read visual arts and now in Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (Kumasi) reading Communication Design My goal is to be a great icon to young designers around the world.
